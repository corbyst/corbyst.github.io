Introducing Rob

A Stable Coin Investor.

Get Started

Rob is an active member of the Decentralized Finance community.

As a sophisticated investor he understands financial risk.



Next

Rob's Search For Returns



Rob is looking for stable returns from his stable coin holdings. Currently they sit in his electronic wallet under utilized.


Next

Rob registers his details so he can get rewarded for helping to support Amazon Brand Sellers.

He goes through a Know Your Customer and Anti Money Laundering appraisal to join the community.



Next

Now he is registered, Rob can pledge his Dai stable coin to the pool.



Rob's Dai is locked into a smart contract. If the pool needs extra capital his Dai is automatically exchanged for Drop (Risk Averse) or Tin (Risk Taker) tokens.

Next

The Tin and Drop tokens gain value as Amazon Sellers pay their Sales Fees.

If inventory levels drop from sales then some of the Drop or Tin tokens are paid down along with their accrued returns.

By putting his Dai to work Rob gets a fair return for his level of risk.

With the smart contract automatically allocating his portion of the Sales Fees Rob's rewards grow along with the Amazon brands he is supporting.
